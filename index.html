Effect of Dryocosmus kuriphilus on the physiology and morphology of Castanea sativa leaves

University of the Aegean internship study examining how chestnut gall wasp infestation affects the physiology and leaf morphology of Castanea sativa across five provenances. Conducted in collaboration with the Forest Research Institute (Entomology Lab & Ecophysiology Lab, ELGO Demeter). Measurements included leaf area, LMA, LDMC, chlorophyll fluorescence, photosynthesis rates, and DCI infestation index. Statistical analysis in R: ANOVA, PCA, mixed-effects modelling.
